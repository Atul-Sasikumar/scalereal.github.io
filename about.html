How we work

We're a remote first company helping people across the to world build and scale their dream projects. We do that by providing services like consulting and building softwares that are needed for the business in cost effective and scalable manner.

We work in async mode so that the best work comes out of everyone

We are big fan of Cal Newport’s DEEP WORK and recommend everyone to read the book.

We believe that with the right tools, you can have big impact with less hassle. Tools are your weapons if remote work is the war.

We over communicate things to make them clear to everyone without presuming any context.

We prefer documentation over talks.

We believe in small teams. Small teams are fast and nimble. Small teams mean less bureaucracy and less management and more getting things done.

We believe in a safe, welcoming, and inclusive environment.

All teammates at ScaleReal agree to a code of conduct.
